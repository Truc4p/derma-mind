## Basic Salon Acne Treatment

The basic salon acne treatment includes these steps.

1. Thoroughly cleanse and analyze. Do not tone the skin. 
2. Cleanse again, this time with a foaming cleanser. 
3. Apply a desincrusting pre-mask. Begin the steam treatment during the premask. Allow the pre-mask to sit for eight minutes. 
4. Remove the pre-mask, unless the product can be used with galvanic current (check the manufacturer's directions). Proceed with galvanic desincrustation as normal; this treatment should take about five minutes. 
5. Begin extraction in the chin area first, moving up the face and finishing at the forehead. This method avoids contact with any blood or fluids. As previously mentioned, the esthetician should wear latex disposable gloves during extraction and throughout the remainder of treatment. 
6. After extraction, apply a generous amount of antibacterial toner to the entire area extracted.
